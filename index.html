◆ Our Method

Our Exclusive 7-Step Cleaning Process

Every carpet cleaned by Diamond undergoes this rigorous seven-step system — developed over a decade of professional experience to achieve the deepest, safest, most thorough clean possible.

01

Pre-Inspection

We inspect the carpet thoroughly to identify fabric type, existing damage, high-traffic areas, and any special staining that requires targeted treatment.

02

Furniture Moving

We carefully move and protect your furniture, placing foam blocks beneath legs to avoid moisture transfer and marks during the cleaning process.

03

Dry Vacuuming

We pre-vacuum every area with high-powered professional equipment to extract loose soil, dust, pet hair and surface debris before applying any solutions.

04

Pre-Treatment

Specialist pre-treatment solutions are applied to high-traffic areas, stains and spots — breaking down grease, oils and embedded soiling at the molecular level.

05

Agitation

The pre-treatment is gently agitated into carpet fibres to ensure deep penetration and optimal activation of cleaning agents before extraction.

06

Hot Water Extraction

Our powerful hot water extraction removes all dissolved soil, chemicals and bacteria, rinsing fibres to a pure, clean state with virtually no residue left behind.

07

Post-Grooming & Dry

Carpets are professionally groomed to restore pile direction and maximise drying efficiency. Fast-drying technology means you're walking on fresh carpets within hours.
